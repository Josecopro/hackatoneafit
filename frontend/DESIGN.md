# design.md - Visual Identity Guide: Alcaldía de Medellín

This document establishes the strict rules for design, composition, and brand usage for the Alcaldía de Medellín and its designation as the District of Science, Technology, and Innovation. All generated graphic pieces must strictly adhere to these parameters.

## 1. Logotype and Brand

The main logo consists of the coat of arms and the institutional text. Its reproduction without following these guidelines is strictly prohibited.

* **Planimetry:** The logo is built on a grid with a proportion of 17x by 9x.
* **Complementary Text:** A vertical space corresponding to 2.5x must be added to include the denomination "Distrito de Ciencia, Tecnología e Innovación".
* **Protection Area (Clear Space):** A minimum security area equivalent to the "X" measurement has been determined. The logo must not be placed over areas of photographs that have visual noise affecting its legibility.
* **Minimum Size:** To guarantee legibility, the minimum width is 3.5 cm for printed media. For digital media, the minimum width is 150 pixels.

## 2. Institutional Typography

The visual language of the brand is transmitted through specific typographic families that guarantee clarity.

* **Logotype:** The exclusive font for the text of the Alcaldía de Medellín logo is Arial Bold.
* **Graphic Pieces and Advertising:** The main typographic family for body text, titles, and general layout is Metropolis. 
* **Usage in Titles:** Titles in all graphic pieces must be short. A maximum of two sizes of the Metropolis font in Bold and Regular variants is allowed.

## 3. Color Palette

The defined colors consolidate the personality of the brand and the connection with the citizens.

* **Flag of Medellin:** Composed of 50% white (#ffffff) at the top and 50% green (#00904c / Pantone 347C) at the bottom.
* **Secondary/Complementary Colors:** The manual defines specific Hex color codes for contrasts and complements. Permitted variations include #2e3e97, #832171, #003A57, #ffdc21, #FCC62B, #00a7b8, and #002A2E.

## 4. Incorrect Uses and Restrictions

To protect the integrity of the institutional image, the following actions represent incorrect uses and are strictly prohibited:

* Deforming, elongating, or changing the shape of the logo.
* Rotating the logo or using gradients.
* Using the coat of arms without its respective institutional text.
* Altering the established colors or typography.
* Including names of secretariats, slogans, or administration phrases directly attached to the logo.

## 5. Layout and Applications

### 5.1. General Logo Placement
* Design elements must respect the protection area, placing the logo in the middle or at the bottom.
* The area reserved for the logo generally corresponds to 1/3 of the horizontal measurement of the piece.
* For digital pieces, the coat of arms must be protected in the center of the piece or on the lower right side.

### 5.2. Co-branding Rules (Shared Brands)
* The Alcaldía de Medellín always closes all communicational pieces.
* **Without Roles:** If there are no hierarchies, logos are placed respecting a space equivalent to the protection area ("X"). No dividing line is used if there are no differentiated roles.
* **With Roles:** A dividing line must be applied to give order to the logos. The dividing line must be scaled to 80% of the height when there is more than one role.
* Accompanying logos must be 100% comparable in visual proportion. Decentralized entities must always be placed on the left side of the Alcaldía.
